HOW WE WORK

LED & Illuminated Signs

Make your business impossible to miss, day or night. Our LED and illuminated signs are engineered for maximum visibility using energy-efficient lighting, durable housings, and vibrant face panels that stand up to Maritime weather. From channel letters and light boxes to full LED message centres, we design and build illuminated signage that draws eyes to your storefront, building, or roadside location around the clock.

    Vehicle & Marine Graphics

    Turn every vehicle in your fleet into a moving billboard. We produce high-impact wraps, partial wraps, and cut-vinyl lettering for cars, trucks, vans, trailers, and boats. Using premium cast vinyl and laminate rated for years of outdoor exposure, your graphics will stay sharp on the highway, at the wharf, or parked outside your shop. From a simple logo and phone number to a full colour wrap, we handle design through installation.

      Large Format Printing

      Go big without sacrificing detail. Our wide-format printers produce vibrant, high-resolution output on banners, coroplast, aluminum composite, foam board, and more. Whether you need a towering roadside banner, a backdrop for your booth, or durable outdoor panels, we print on the right material for the job and finish it with UV-protective laminate so colours stay true season after season.

        Window & Wall Graphics

        Transform glass and bare walls into branded real estate. We produce perforated window film, frosted vinyl, full-colour murals, and precision-cut decals that turn your storefront or interior into a professional, eye-catching space. Whether you want full privacy film, seasonal promotions, or a permanent brand statement, our materials are designed for clean removal when you're ready for a refresh.

          3 Dimensional Lettering

          Add depth and presence to your brand with dimensional lettering. CNC-routed from HDU foam, acrylic, aluminum, or PVC, these letters and logos mount directly to your building facade, lobby wall, or monument sign for a polished, professional look. Available painted, metallic-finished, or backlit, 3D letters give your business a level of quality that flat signage simply can't match.

            A-Frame & Wayfinding

            Guide customers right to your door. Our A-frame sidewalk signs, directional posts, and wayfinding systems are built to handle foot traffic and weather alike. Perfect for restaurants, retail shops, campgrounds, marinas, and municipal properties, these signs are designed for clarity at a glance, helping people find exactly where they need to go while reinforcing your brand at every turn.

              Stickers & Magnets

              Small but mighty. Custom stickers and vehicle magnets are one of the most cost-effective ways to get your brand out into the world. We print on durable vinyl with a protective laminate so your stickers hold up outdoors, and our magnets let you brand a personal vehicle for work hours and remove it when you're off the clock. Great for promotions, giveaways, product labels, and fleet branding on a budget.

                Custom Cut Signage

                Stand out with signage shaped to your vision. Using precision CNC routing and contour cutting, we produce custom-shaped signs from aluminum composite, PVC, HDU, and acrylic. Everything from shaped business signs and hanging blade signs to decorative panels and themed displays. If you can imagine the shape, we can cut it, print it, and finish it to last.

                  Event & Trade Show Signage

                  Make a big impression at your next event. We produce retractable banner stands, pop-up backdrops, table throws, tent graphics, and custom displays that travel easy and set up fast. Whether it's a trade show in Halifax, a community festival, or a corporate event, our event signage is designed to look sharp under any lighting and fold down for the next show.
